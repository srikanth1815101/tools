Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: May 26, 2025

Last Updated: May 26, 2025

At CSRGO Tools (tools.csrgo.com), we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We strive to continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

1. Our Commitment

We are committed to making our website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

  • Ensuring our website is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust
  • Providing alternative text for images and non-text content
  • Maintaining sufficient color contrast for text readability
  • Ensuring keyboard navigation is possible throughout the site

2. Accessibility Features

Our website includes several accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Descriptive link text
  • Alternative text for images

3. Browser Compatibility

Our website is designed to work with the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome (latest version)
  • Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
  • Safari (latest version)
  • Microsoft Edge (latest version)

4. Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure that our website is accessible, we acknowledge that there may be some limitations:

  • Some third-party content may not be fully accessible
  • Older content may not meet current accessibility standards
  • Some complex tools may require additional accessibility considerations

5. Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • contact.csrgo@gmail.com
  • tools.csrgo.com

6. Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving the accessibility of our website. We regularly review our content and make necessary updates to ensure compliance with accessibility standards.
