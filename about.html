Privacy-First Approach

Our website is fully static and operates entirely within your browser. We do not collect, process, or store any user data on our servers — because we don't have any servers. We do not require account creation, and we do not use forms that gather personal information, except when users voluntarily contact us.

In the future, we may display Google AdSense advertisements. These are subject to Google's data policies, and we advise users to review our Privacy Policy for more information.

Operated From India

CSRGO Tools is proudly created and operated from India. We comply with applicable Indian laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and relevant data protection regulations. We are committed to transparency, user safety, and accessibility for all.
